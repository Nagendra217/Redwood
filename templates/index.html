About US

Genesis

Founded in 2015, RW Investment advisors is a SEBI registered investment advisory firm (Registration No. INA200004342) run by first generation entrepreneurs who have a combined investment experience of 25 years and an extraordinary passion for public markets.

Mandate

We are a sector and horizon agnostic advisory firm with deep local knowledge and network. We advise our clients on investing in stable businesses with a track record of excellence in capital allocation, governance, management bandwidth and ethical conduct.

Safety First

As advisors, we believe in disciplined and patient approach to investing irrespective of market cycles and complete alignment of interests with our partners and stakeholders.

Philosophy

At RW investment advisors, we are firm believers of India's long term economic fundamentals driven by a young and aspiring middle-class, sound educational and legal institutions and a vibrant democracy. Equities is the right conduit to participate in India's growth.


We specialize in public market investments and have a proprietary framework which combines fundamental and technical factors to identify businesses that can create long term wealth. Our guiding philosophy is protection of capital and compounding of value over longer periods.


We are staunch followers of Buffett, i.e. growth at reasonable price philosophy and look for quantifiable moats in the companies that we invest in. One of the key reasons to start RW investment advisors was to get away from the crowd, keep noise at bay and focus on longer term trends.

Chaitanya Jagarlapudi

Prior to founding RW Advisors, Chaitanya was a Vice President at Argonaut Private Equity, a diversified global private equity fund with more than $ 6 bn under management; leading their BFSI vertical and specializing in identifying mid-cap opportunities. In a career spanning 12 years in the investing field, he was also an investment banker at Lazard India and an equity research analyst at Irevna Research covering technology stocks in the US.


Chaitanya is a bookworm addicted to reading especially books on investing, science and philosophy. He strongly believes that investing is very similar to living ones life wisely - which calls for patience and choosing your battles wisely. He has an MBA from ISB and is a chemical engineer from IIT Madras.

Saikiran Pulavarthi

Prior to founding RW Advisors, Sai was Head of Research and BFSI Sector lead at Espirito Santo Securities, a boutique equity research outfit focused on mid-cap research in the Indian Public Markets ; He has 13 years of professional experience both on the sell-side and buy side at organizations including Fidelity, Centrum and Standard Chartered Bank. He is a Chartered Accountant and a Company Secretary by qualification.


In his down time Sai is an avid reader with interests spanning global businesses, history, politics and Indian philosophy. He firmly believes that evolution of an individual, society or an organisation depends on its history and helps in predicting the future

Anil Khatod

Anil is one of three partners at Argonaut and has deep experience, both on the operating and investing areas. In 1980, Anil co-founded a venture back liquid crystal display company in Sunnyvale, California which was acquired in 1982. He subsequently joined Nortel Networks where he served in various senior executive roles leading upto the role of Chief Marketing & Strategy Officer for the company.


Just Prior to joining Argonaut, Anil was the CEO of AirDefence, a market leader in wireless network security with more than 500 enterprise customers globally. Anil currently serves on the board of Memjet, Venture East Proactive, Siklu Networks and OmniGuide Inc. Over the last 10 years, Anil has also been an angel investor with investments ranging from bio fuels to healthcare to technology companies. Anil has a bachelor and masters degree in business administration from the university of Rajasthan India and is a Chartered Accountant from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India.
